DREAM PEOPLE
They met on Facebook in 2018. In 2019 they released the acclaimed single, "Forever, Too Long", and in early 2020, "Soft Violence", their debut EP that took them to many emblematic stages - such as Vodafone Paredes de Coura Festival and Super Bock em Stock - and earned them the attention of Portuguese and foreign media. In 2020 they suffered a major setback when all of their accounts were hacked and deleted. However, in a baffling demonstration of solidarity from the portuguese music community they managed not only to recover but to double their fan base. After the storm, the bonanza: from January 2021 Dream People will release “Almost Young” the band's second EP and the most personal to date. Although they have "dream" in their name they play more than dream pop. Listening to their songs we may find hints of post rock, indie rock and even electronic music, their biggest influences being Radiohead, Slowdive and Jeff Buckley. "Keep Dream People in your drawer (...) next to the Cocteau Twins, Slowdive and Cigarettes After Sex (...)" - Arte-Factos
